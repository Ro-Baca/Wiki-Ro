# Descripción 

Escribe una historia completa utilizando exactamente 55 palabra, debe tener:
- Almenos un personaje
- Un conflicto 
- Crear un escenario o setting
- Necesita una resolucion (o un twist)

Reglas a considerar:
- Palabras con Hyphen =  1 Palabra
- Contracciones = 1 Palabra
- Abreviaciones y formas cortas = 1 palabra
- Acronimos = 1 palabra
- Numeros = 1 palabra
- El titulo no forma parte del conteo 
- La puntuaciòn no forma parte del conteo 

>[!Tip]  Primero escribe toda la historia, despues puedes editar y remover los excedentes.

# On a New York Street

On a New York street a man runs, late for work. He pushes some people blocking his way, stops at a window to fix his hat and tie, and then keeps running. Passing by a coffee shop he takes a cup. At his workplace he sits and starts asking for change, pointing at his cup.
